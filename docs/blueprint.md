# **App Name**: LinguaTune

## Core Features:

- Story Generation: Generate short stories based on user-selected level of difficulty in their native language.
- Language Translation: Translate the story into the user-selected foreign language. Powered by an AI translation tool for accuracy and nuance.
- Text-to-Speech (TTS): Generate an MP3 audio file of the translated story using TTS. Match the spoken cadence of native speakers.
- Karaoke-Style Display: Display the translated story on the screen with a karaoke-style highlighting bar that progresses in sync with the MP3 audio.  The app calculates timing based on word count and audio length.
- Play/Pause Controls: Simple UI to play and pause the audio and highlighting.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#3498DB) to evoke feelings of clarity, focus, and intellectual openness.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECF0F1), a calm backdrop that minimizes distractions.
- Accent color: Orange (#E67E22) for interactive elements such as the play/pause button and the karaoke highlight bar, promoting a sense of active engagement.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for a modern and neutral look.
- Use simple, clear icons for the play/pause controls and language selection, ensuring ease of use and quick comprehension.
- Employ a clean, intuitive layout that puts the focus on the story text and karaoke bar, reducing visual clutter to facilitate easy reading and language acquisition.
- Subtle, smooth animations for the karaoke highlighting to guide the user's reading and maintain an engaging learning experience without being distracting.
